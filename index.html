"I took a blank slate as an invitation to build something that truly serves Yale College. With no preset boundaries, I forged new collaborations, led strategic projects, and integrated communities across the university."

My work sits at the intersection of technology, people, and institutional change. I've spent my career in higher ed IT not just keeping systems running, but asking why they exist, who they serve, and how they could work better. That means building AI communities of practice, migrating 50+ websites, deploying POS systems across residential colleges, automating workflows that used to eat staff hours, and producing a podcast that gives a real voice to the Yale community.

I'm also a mentor, a researcher, and a connector. The throughline in everything I do is the same: good technology, built with intention, changes lives.
